A properly functioning digestive system is essential to your overall health and happiness.

Consequently, any related disorder requires immediate care from compassionate, trusted specialists.

For nearly 40 years, the doctors and staff of Gastroenterology Associates (GA) have been providing personalized, quality care to patients experiencing a wide range of digestive disorders. Our team of highly trained specialists takes pride in ensuring that each and every patient is treated with the care, personal attention, and respect they deserve.

For patients’ convenience, we are located adjacent to Long Island Center for Digestive Health (LICDH), a New York State-licensed ambulatory endoscopy center that has been awarded a Certificate of Recognition from the American Society of Gastrointestinal Endoscopy and is accredited by the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care.

Together, Gastroenterology Associates and Long Island Center for Digestive Health combine the experience and compassion of trusted digestive health professionals with the comfort, convenience and personalized care of a renowned facility to administer an unparalleled caliber of digestive healthcare to all patients.

Restore your digestive system to its optimal health and get back to living again with the leaders in advanced digestive healthcare.

At Gastroenterology Associates, we take digestive health seriously. We know that no digestive disorder is pleasant, and we understand that it can often be an embarrassing and stressful experience.

Our team of highly trained digestive experts possess the compassion, competency and qualifications necessary to diagnose and remedy a wide range of digestive issues, and we are committed to improving your quality of life and restoring you to maximum health.

This strong devotion to our patients is but one characteristic that sets us apart from other practices. Another is our special relationship with Long Island Center for Digestive Health (LICDH), a non-hospital outpatient facility equipped with the tools and technology to conduct advanced diagnostic testing and procedures.

LICDH is more comfortable than a hospital, and located in the same building as Gastroenterology Associates—so your doctors, nurses, technicians and anesthesiologists are all under one roof. Among other benefits, this streamlines the scheduling process and ensures that you’ll always see a familiar face when visiting Gastroenterology Associates and Long Island Center for Digestive Health—familiarity that can go a long way to calming the nerves and providing much-needed peace of mind.

That’s “The GA Difference.”

Gastroenterology Associates (GA) specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of all types of gastrointestinal, liver, pancreatic and gallbladder diseases. Serving the North and South shores of Long Island, (including: Central Nassau County, Garden City, Mineola, Westbury, East Meadow, Hempstead, New Hyde Park, Hicksville, Levittown, Floral Park, Rockville Centre and Williston Park.) We have helped thousands of people by providing care for digestive diseases, colon cancer screening, IBS and IBD (ulcerative colitis, Crohn’s disease), GERD, heartburn and acid reflux, swallowing disorders, liver, pancreatic and gallbladder disease, ulcers, celiac disease and H. pylori.
