Who We Are

 

Founded in 1987, Versailles Group is a boutique investment bank that specializes in mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, private placements, and Fairness Opinions.

For 30+ years, Versailles Group has created value for its clients. Versailles Group has advised on the sale or purchase of numerous niche market businesses with revenues greater than US$2 million, literally around the world.

Versailles Group’s senior level bankers have consistently proven that they can successfully close transactions that meet or exceed demanding clients’ expectations.
