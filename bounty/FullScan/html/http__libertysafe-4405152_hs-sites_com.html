About Us

Liberty Safe's market leadership is founded on high quality, innovative products offering superior fire protection, technologically advanced security, exquisite styling and functional storage capacity. Its products are sold through a nationwide network, providing expert sales and service. Liberty Safe offers a limited lifetime replacement warranty on its products.

Founded in 1988, Liberty Safe is headquartered in Payson, Utah. Liberty safe employs approximately 500.
