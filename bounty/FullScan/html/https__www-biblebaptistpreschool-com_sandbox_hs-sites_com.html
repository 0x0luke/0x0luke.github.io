A Great Preschool Located In Your Backyard

Bible Baptist Preschool provides exceptional services to the families who become part of our preschool family.  We are located 1 mile off Independence Boulevard on Margaret Wallace Road in Matthews, NC.  

Why We Chose Bible Baptist Preschool

Bible Baptist was recommended by a family at my church. My wife and I find the care to be exceptional. Most importantly, there is Christian based teachings in their curriculum. I love it when my daughter is on the car ride home and I hear her praising the Lord with a new song she learned in class. Also, the teachers are wonderful and are always attentive. We find the program to be a true blessing.

- Luis Ramirez, Parent

We chose Bible Baptist because of the inclusion of God in the curriculum. While other educational systems are pushing God out, Bible Baptist is embracing our creator and reinforcing what we (parents) are teaching at home. What we really love about Bible Baptist is the care and time that each teacher and the staff invests into each student.

-Anthony Davis, Parent

Our Mission and Faith

Bible Baptist Preschool seeks to help families lovingly care and train their children socially, educationally, physically, and spiritually.  We strongly encourage children to love and forgive each other, to be kind and caring towards others, and to be obedient and respectful towards authority.
