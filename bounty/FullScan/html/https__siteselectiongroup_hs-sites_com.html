Economic Incentive Management Software

Corporations needlessly suffer millions of dollars in lost economic incentives due to the failure to adhere to compliance requirements. To minimize these losses, Site Selection Group developed IncenTrak® which is one of the only platforms on the market that enables you to maximize economic incentive value by giving you control of the compliance process.

Download Our LaborCast® Brochure

LaborCast® is a cutting-edge workforce supply and demand forecasting tool that enables you to develop long term labor plans for your existing and planned operations. This forward looking product evaluates the labor supply and demand equilibrium for your targeted employees to optimize your ability to make informed labor market decisions.

Download Our GeoCision® Brochure

Our GeoCision® location benchmarking tool will enable you to evaluate locations across the world to help you stay competitive in today’s global marketplace. It evaluates a variety of location factors including labor availability, labor costs, geo-political risks, infrastructure quality, real estate conditions, utility costs and business climate.
