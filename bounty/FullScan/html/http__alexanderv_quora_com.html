Details
Life as seen through the eyes of a young male.  Posting at least every Monday and Thursday. 
 
Initially, writing was a way of self-therapy and exploration.  Then it transitioned to an expression of myself and my weekend.  Now, it is the third-person expression of my life. 
 
The events are transposed and stylized, characters are created to protect identities, but know that I write about my life. 
 
To explore my life.  To explore things unspoken, thoughts unsaid, and pains unaddressed.  Through my writing, I seek to help and inspire those in similar situations.
 
If you take five minutes out of your day to read my posts, to understand my struggle, to relate to my life, I truly appreciate it.
