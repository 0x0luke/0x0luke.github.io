    More than 5,000 patients have trusted 

    InVia with their fertility treatment.

    InVia Fertility Specialists is a state-of-the-art Chicago fertility clinic with a strong patient-centered focus. Our highly trained staff understand the importance of maintaining a supportive and comfortable environment for each patient.

    We have four convenient Chicago fertility clinics in the north and northwest suburbs.
    There we offer a full range of assisted reproductive technologies including non-donor and donor egg IVF, egg freezing, intrauterine (artificial) insemination, pre-implantation genetic diagnosis, assisted zona hatching, intracytoplasmic sperm injection, testicular sperm aspiration, fertility surgery, and more.

    Our goal is the same as yours; to achieve a timely and successful pregnancy. We are honored to help you in your pathway to pregnancy.
