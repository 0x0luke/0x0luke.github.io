Why Us?

We’ve been in business since 2003. We know what businesses need to successfully manage their people, so their people can manage the business. Fuse is committed to offering an excellent customer experience to help whenever needed.

What to Expect

We work side by side with our customers to make sure they get set up successfully on our platform. Once you’re up and running, you’ll have full access to our support team to help you get everything you need out of the Fuse platform.

Support

Our support is second to none. We work with our customers to make sure they and each of their employees can successfully manage all of their HR needs from Fuse.
        

Cloud-Based

Access your system anytime, anywhere. Whether you’re traveling, working remotely, or out sick, you have 24/7/365 access to the applications. All you need is a web browser or mobile device. You remain in constant control of your business information.

Real-Time Data

Get in-the-moment access to your data. The suite effectively eliminates the guesswork of labor expenses by seamlessly integrating HR, Payroll, and Time and Labor Management, allowing data to be viewed and analyzed in real time. This enables you to see the full picture of your operations whenever you need to.

Customizable

No two industries are the same, so to help meet your needs for a workforce management platform, Fuse offers a marketplace of third-party tools. These tools can extend what you can do with the platform and provide more customization for your companies’ needs.
