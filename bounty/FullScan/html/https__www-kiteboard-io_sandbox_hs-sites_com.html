Highlights
Small Size, Large Performance
50 mm x 70 mm low profile board including powerful 1.2 GHz Quad Core ARM Cortex A53 processor, complete connectivity, location, multimedia capabilities
Cellular Enabled
Directly connect to the internet with 4G speeds. Stream videos and more. Supports fallback to 3G/2G, 2 SIM cards (DSDS), Data, Voice calls & SMS
Android Ready
Build your own device with Android. Use standard application development to quickly build rich application-centric devices that include connectivity, multimedia, contextual awareness and intelligence
Eminently Extendable
Low speed connector with UART/I2C/SPI and GPIOs to interface anything. Develop your own add on board and jump from prototype to production
Power Optimized
Low sleep current with network connection. Run off standard LiPoly batteries. Qualcomm Quickcharge 2.0 plus dedicated DC charging for maximum performance and flexibility
Rich Peripherals
Separate high speed connectors for MIPI display (upto 1080p + touch support) and up-to two cameras. Jumpstart development with autofocus enabled 8 MP camera, 5" touch display and more
