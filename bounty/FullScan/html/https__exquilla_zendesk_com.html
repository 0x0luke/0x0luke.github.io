ExQuilla

Microsoft Exchange Server email for Mozilla Thunderbird

About ExQuilla

ExQuilla is an addon for the Mozilla Thunderbird email client that allows you to access both your emails and contacts stored on an Exchange server. ExQuilla uses EWS (Exchange Web Services) for access to the Exchange server.

ExQuilla is not free software, but is licensed on an annual basis. New users are granted a free trial license for a limited time automatically. After that, a purchase is required.
