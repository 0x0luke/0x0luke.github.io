A Community Bank With A Big Commitment To Service

State Bank of Chilton is a small community bank with the resources and knowledge to help our customers achieve big financial goals. Offering free checking accounts and convenient online banking, we can deliver what you need with personal, professional service.

From checking accounts that keep your finances running smoothly, to individually serviced investment accounts, small business loans and mortgages that allow you to make your dreams a reality; we are proud to offer every customer exceptional service. Whether you need your first free checking account or your retirement plan, working with a local bank offers many advantages. Get started on your successful financial journey with State Bank of Chilton, today.
