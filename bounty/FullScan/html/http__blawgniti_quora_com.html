Blawgniti is a blog started to write about Indian laws, enforcement and other allied topics.

'Blawgniti' combines the words 'Blog', 'Law' and 'Niti', a Sanskrit word which means 'Ethics / Rule of law'.

Disclaimer -

This blog does not aim to be a source of advertising. It is intended to provide information and share knowledge of Indian laws.
The information shared will be general in nature and is not intended to be a substitute for professional legal advice. No attorney client relationship will therefore result from accessing this blog.

You are requested to seek professional help and not act on any information provided here in case you need.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The focus will be laws in India.

I will also try and write about Jurisprudence, ethics and philosophy of law. More than the rules and procedure, it is the philosophy that interests me.

I will be happy to allow guest posts provided I feel they are of the requisite quality. I reserve a right to edit and post them.

The copyright of this blog is owned solely by me and the posts that will feature will not be meant for reproduction. By making a post to this blog, you agree to all of the terms mentioned here.

I will also be happy to provide answers to specific legal questions. You can ask them through a post here. Or you can personally message me.
