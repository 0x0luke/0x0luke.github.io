Playfire Has Moved to Green Man Gaming

In order to provide Playfire gamers the absolute best platform to continue as a community we have migrated Playfire over to Green Man Gaming. Over the course of the coming months we will be adding many of the original Playfire features to our new community platform and some fresh ideas we’re sure you’ll enjoy.

We are very grateful to the members of Playfire who choose to move to the new platform and if you are a Playfire veteran you will receive an exclusive Veteran badge on Green Man Gaming and the Playfire profile theme when badges and themes are introduced later this year. Playfire lives on in a new home, and we hope to see you there.

For support with our Playfire Client, please contact our Customer Service team over at Green Man Gaming Support.
