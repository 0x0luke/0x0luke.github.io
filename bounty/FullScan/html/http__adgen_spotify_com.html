Any Questions?

How and when are ads served on Spotify?

Listeners on our free, ad-supported platform hear 15- or 30-second audio ads at naturally occurring breaks between songs during their listening session.

How do I create an audio ad?

Our voiceover feature offers start-to-finish audio ad creation directly within Ad Studio. After inputting your script, you’ll select a background track and voice profile and we’ll take it from there. What’s more, it’s free of charge! Learn more about how it works here.

How do I get started?

From adstudio.spotify.com, select the Sign up button and follow the steps to sign up.

How much does it cost?

The costs of your ad campaign vary depending on your targeting selections. You can build a campaign with a minimum $250 budget.

What is Spotify Ad Studio?

Ad Studio is Spotify’s new, self-service advertising platform that makes it easy for anyone to create and manage Spotify audio ad campaigns directly. With Ad Studio, you can:

  • Build an audio ad campaign in less than 10 minutes
  • Create a new audio ad from scratch
  • Forecast impression estimates based on your target audience
  • Track and manage all your campaign reporting
