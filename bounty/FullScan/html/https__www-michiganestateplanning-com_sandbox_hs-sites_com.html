About Christopher J. Berry

Certified Elder Law Attorney, Estate Planner, Holistic Planning Advisor & Founder of the Elder Care Firm



Chris is a Certified Elder Law Attorney who has spent the last 14+ years helping secure the futures of his clients and their families — from helping them protect their estate, assets, and retirement savings, to minimizing their long-term care costs so they can pass on a legacy they are proud of.

Between Chris and the hard work of his team, the Elder Care Firm now serves 6 locations across the Detroit Metro area and has grown into one of the top estate planning and elder law firms in Michigan.
