Events

Throughout the year, the LSU Alumni Association welcomes thousands of alums back to campus for a variety of events. From the popular Tiger Alumni Band Reunion in the fall to Hall of Distinction induction ceremonies in the spring, the LSU Alumni Association holds dozens of different events to both honor and entertain past students of LSU.
