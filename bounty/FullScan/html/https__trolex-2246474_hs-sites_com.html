Welcome to Trolex - Leading safety technology

We design and manufacture innovative safety products for gas and dust detection, connector solutions and strata monitoring systems in the hazardous industrial, mining and tunnelling markets.

We are a progressive technology group which aims to become the leading name in safety technology, wherever and whenever workers operate in challenging environments. We bring to market products that are dramatically superior to our competition, providing outstanding real-world benefits to our customers.

MINING

We offer a full suite of protection and monitoring products and systems to the mining industry, allowing our customers to effectively command their mine, and to maximize productivity and safety.

INDUSTRIAL

We specialize in advanced, intelligent, rapidly deployable gas and dust detection systems as well as Ex connector and portable gas detection products with very low cost maintenance.

TUNNELLING

Our dedicated tunnelling division supplies bespoke systems and solutions including gas and dust detection, Ex connectors and tunnel boring-machine monitoring systems, supporting major projects around the world.

OIL & GAS

We supply robust and rapidly deployable low-maintenance technologies for the Oil and Gas sector, including personal gas detectors and our range of Ex Connectors, helping you to minimise downtime and maximise worker safety.

HOW TO PROPERLY PROTECT YOUR WORKERS FROM LONG-TERM DISEASES

The risk of toxic airborne particulates for workers in harsh and hazardous environments is not a new one. But why are organisations still struggling to implement adequate protective measures? In our eBook, we reveal why standard particulate monitoring methods are flawed and the importance of achieving continuous, real-time monitoring to proactively manage the wellbeing of your workforce.
