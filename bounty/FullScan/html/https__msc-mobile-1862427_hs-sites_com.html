Carl Stahl's Customer Service 4.0

The vision of Carl Stahl goes way beyond mobilizing their customer service. It is the goal to offer a fully integrated customer service experience to their clients. This includes a modern SAM Smart Asset Management based mobile application, a portal where customers can see the inspection results of their equipments and their own RFID tags and readers.
 
 
 

30 000 travelers with SAP on iPad and counting

When our customer - a international pharmaceutical company from Switzerland - replaced their sales reps laptops with iPads, they needed to run SAP Travel Management on them too. Our Travel Plus solution that connects to SAP Travel Management on premise is now helping more than 30 000 users world wide to do their travel management on their iPads.
 

 

Mobile Sales with Sales Plus for SAP ERP at Zedach

Sales Plus for SAP ERP - running on the SAP Mobile Platform - helps Zedach to optimize the sales of their 150 reps in the field. It gives them offline access to their customers, contacts, products and activities. They can create sales orders on the go and directly synchronize them to their SAP ERP system.
 
 

1.7 GB of data offline on iPads with SAP MobiLink

SAP's MobiLink technology is a robust and scalable way to synchronize high amounts of data to mobile devices of all kinds. In one of our latest projects we synchronized more than 1.7 GB of data to iPads and Windows 10 tablets. This included complex pricing and variant configuration data.
