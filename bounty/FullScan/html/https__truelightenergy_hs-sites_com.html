    A MESSAGE FROM MIKE

    The energy industry is incredibly complex.  For industry participants, the difference between winning and losing is often based on how well they manage and interpret the avalanche of data that hits their systems every single day.

     

    That’s why I founded TRUELight Energy in 2011.  I saw the need for independent, high quality energy data and analytics that helps bring clarity and transparency to energy markets. 

     

    We help our clients get it right.  Our market expertise and industry best data, models and analytics provide the visibility and certainty that our clients are making their decisions based on the best available information.

     

    In short, we like to call it “The Power of Transparency.”
