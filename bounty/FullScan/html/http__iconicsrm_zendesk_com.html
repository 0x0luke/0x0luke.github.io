THE ICONIC is pleased to greet you as a valued partner. To guarantee that the delivery into our Fulfillment Centre works smoothly for both parties and ensures we remain true to our customers, your compliance with THE ICONIC Delivery Instructions described here is essential.

The commercial relationship between THE ICONIC and our suppliers shall be governed and read in conjunction with THE ICONIC’s Terms and Conditions of Purchase. The Terms and Conditions cover key commercial aspects of THE ICONIC's trading relationships, such as payment terms, circumstances in which goods may be refused by reason of defects, and the consequences of late and non-compliant deliveries.

It is a condition of supplying to THE ICONIC that our Terms and Conditions and Mandatory Delivery Instructions described here are adhered to. By using this service, you indicate your acceptance of these Terms and Conditions.

IMPORTANT: DELIVERY REFUSAL GUIDELINES

18th July 2019

Dear Trading Partner,

Please note: The below communication is applicable to ICONIC vendors who are operating under DDP (Direct Duty Paid) incoterm. If you are NOT operating under a DDP incoterm agreement, please view THE ICONIC Operational Guidelines (link here) and disregard the below Delivery Refusal Guidelines.

THE ICONIC has and continues to experience rapid growth over the years. In order to scale and support our growth, a few exciting initiatives are launching in our business. We are pleased to share with you our Operational Guidelines for our Wholesale Suppliers (‘Operational Guidelines’) which replace any Mandatory Delivery Instructions previously referenced in our agreement or otherwise provided to you.

The Operational Guidelines (link here) detail the operational aspects of our commercial relationship which we require that you adhere to when trading with THE ICONIC, so together we can continue to offer our customers an unparalleled selection of brands and also create a seamless and inspiring experience for our customers.

You will see that a series of improvements have been made to clarify how we accept deliveries into our Fulfillment Center. It is important that you take the time to review these Operational Guidelines with your Logistics/Warehouse department and Carrier(s) to ensure compliance with the conditions outlined in the Operational Guidelines - and to avoid your deliveries being rejected at the dock and requiring re-delivering.

As a key and valued supplier to THE ICONIC, we would like to give your business advance notification that the Delivery Refusal Guidelines will come into effect on Monday 3 September 2019 . An implementation plan with timelines is also detailed below.

Should you have any queries in relation to the Operational Guideline, or require any other assistance, please contact THE ICONIC Vendor Management team via email: VM@theiconic.com.au.

Yours sincerely,

Acting CEO & CCO

Anna Lee & Mareile Osthus

The ICONIC
