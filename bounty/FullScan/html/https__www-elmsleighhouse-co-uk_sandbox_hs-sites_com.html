FAQs

We’re here to provide you with the best dental care in the most comfortable way. Our frequently asked questions provide you with the key information about visiting our clinic and what to expect during your treatment. Our dental team

We are proud to have a team of dentists, hygienists, and technicians at the top of their profession to welcome you and provide the highest standards of care. Led by Tim Thackrah, with more than 28 years experience in implant dentistry, each member is an expert in their field. Our team’s clinical excellence is complemented by their use of the best and most technologically advanced equipment, materials and techniques in oral healthcare.
 

dentist referrals

More than 500 dentists refer their patients to our expert team for dental implants, specialist dentistry, and complex treatments. Referred patients experience the highest quality treatment before being returned to the care of their existing dentist. We work closely with our referring dentists and aim to be an invaluable part of their team. In addition, we welcome new referrals and can offer support and advice through courses and seminars, ensuring all patients experience excellence.
