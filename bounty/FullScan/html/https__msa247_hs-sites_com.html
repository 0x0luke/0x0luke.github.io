Municipal Services of America  is a local professional contractor specializing in a full range of services which include: 

  • Sewers  Cleaning, New Installations, Repairs, Replacements, Sewer Dye Testing for Point of Sale Violations, Sump Pumps, Back-Flow Preventers, Sewer Jetting, Televising, Tee Locations and much more.
  • Concrete Traditional or Stamped Patios, Driveways, Fire Pits, Walkways, Steps, Garage Slabs, Foundations and a Variety of Colors and Finishes.
  • Basement Waterproofing  We offer Interior or Exterior Solutions depending on the Causes we will determine a Remedy.

 

As a third-generation business, we have the experience and commitment to provide work that exceeds expectations. We employ well trained staff and also invest in top of the line equipment to ensure customers get efficient and quality work.  No matter how small or large the job may be, we take pride in doing things the right way.

Throughout the process, you can take comfort in knowing that your job is being cared for by a group of experienced professionals that are here to serve your needs. By working with us, you will quickly see why so many prefer working with Municipal Services of America.
