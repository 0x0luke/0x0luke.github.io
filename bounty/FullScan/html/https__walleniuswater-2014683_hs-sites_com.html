Biocides are a major health risk

Adding hazarduous biocides has been the traditional method of stopping bacterial growth and securing the production. Biocides are strong toxins that kill all living things in the fluid but they also risk affecting the health and safety of all employees in the workshop

The most commonly used biocides often have one or more of the following properties:

  • Carcinogenic and mutagenic
  • Allergenic
  • Toxic or harmful in contact with skin or if inhaled

FluidWorker 100 Our latest addition to the FluidWorker family

FluidWorker 100 is a compact and complete system for controlling and preventing bacterial growth in process fluids. Installation of FluidWorker in your system is quick and easy and doesn't require any stop in production.  Thanks to the patented cleaning function the system is maintenance free.

  • Proven and non-toxic method
  • Proactive bacterial reduction
