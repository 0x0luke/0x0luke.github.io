NEW! Setra Lite™ Pressure Indicator

Green means good to go and red indicates an alarm. Setra has created the simplest and easiest to use visual room pressure monitor in the market. Setra Lite™ is designed to easily fit in a single gang electric box, and also provides an optional visual display of the pressure, powered by Setra's legendary capacitive pressure sensor. Best of all, Setra Lite can tie into your building management systems and has a plug-and-play connection with our Setra FLEX™ Environmental Room Monitor.
