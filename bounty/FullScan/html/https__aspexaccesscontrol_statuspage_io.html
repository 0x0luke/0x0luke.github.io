Migration internet load balancers Aug 22, 21:00 - Aug 23, 04:00 CEST
Update - The migration of the internet Load Balancer has been moved to the earlier communicated maintenance window of Thursday, August 22, 2019. Impact and timings stay as communicated.
Aug 6, 11:32 CEST
Scheduled - On Saturday, August 17, 2019 we will migrate the internet load balancers to the new datacenter, this is also a major step in the datacenter migration project.
The internet load balancers from the new datacenter will be placed in production while the load balancers from the current datacenter will be decommissioned.
During the migration end-users will be disconnected and will not be able to (re)connect until the load balancer in the new datacenter is up and running. Our network provider will start at 9PM and we expect to be operational at 11PM. From 11PM until 4AM testing will be performed and corrective actions may interrupt end-user sessions.
