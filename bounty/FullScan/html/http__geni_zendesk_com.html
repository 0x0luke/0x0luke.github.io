Getting Started

Thanks for visiting the Geni help center.

  • Search

    Do a search for the topic you are inquiring about. Chances are someone has already provided an answer!

    Watch our Video Tutorials for instructions on how to use great features on Geni.

  • Have a question?

    Check out our great Help Topics to find the answers to our most frequently asked questions, or Ask the Community to get help from other Geni users.

  • Submitting a ticket

    Are you still unable to find your answer? Submit a ticket and get a direct response from our expert customer service team.

    Note: The ability to submit tickets is a Geni Plus and Pro only feature. If you are a basic user, please take advantage of our self-help tools, such as Help Topics, Video Tutorials, and our Community Help forums.
