Homepage

 

 

quantilope was founded at the University of Hamburg in 2014 as a global Agile Insights Software. We automate advanced market research projects, allowing clients to go from project design to data collection, analysis, and visualization in less than one week, using complex methods on a single platform. quantilope empowers our clients to embrace agile research processes and to free all business decision from gut feel.

Available methods including MaxDiff, Conjoint, A/B Tests, Implicit Association Tests, Van Westendorp, NPS®, Driver Analysis, and more. Each project can be customized based on individual needs. Average turnaround time, based on project complexity, is 3 - 5 business days. Each client is empowered with a dedicated research consultant to help them get closer to their consumers by answer questions along each dimension of a product's marketing mix.
