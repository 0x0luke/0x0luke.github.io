From script to screen, video to animation, and everything in-between, we are a full service video production company.

NextThought Studios is a leader among video production companies across the United States. Our productions team creates award-winning videos for education, marketing, training & HR, and more.

What can NextThought Studios do for you?

 Animation & 3D Motion Graphics

Animated films aren’t just for kids anymore — now, they're essential tools anyone can use for training and marketing videos alike.

Commercials & TV Spots

Whether we’re enjoying our nightly TV show or gathered around the screen for the big game, commercials have become an expected, and sometimes celebrated, part of American entertainment.

 Corporate Training Videos

Corporate training video production can make your onboarding and continuing education process easier, more affordable and more engaging for your team members.

 Documentaries

Storytelling is a fine art, and nowhere is that more on display than in today’s documentary films.

 Drone Videography

Drone video services can provide a bird’s eye view of what matters most to you.

 Educational Videos

Delivering quality instructional videos for universities around the country is what has allowed us to become the national leader in educational video production.

 Interactive Video

Interactive videos are like "choose your adventure" storybooks. It allows your audience to choose their content and arrive to the training or products they need faster.

 Time Lapse Video

Time lapse video allows you to show a progressive sequence of events and keep your video under two minutes long— the preferred length for viewer engagement.

White Label Video

NextThought Studios provides white label video production services to agencies across the United States.
