about

When you’re in the business of providing a service then “service” had better mean exactly that. And, the best service possible; delivering what you promise in fulfilling the client’s needs - and then some.

When Utility Survey Corp. started out in 1989 not much thought was given as to where the company might be one-quarter-of-a-century down the road. Who really thinks that far ahead?

The focus was on what could be done in the immediate to serve clients, both large and small, in the best way possible to win their trust and loyalty. Now, almost thirty years later our focus remains the same; Service, Service, Service! And, we’re very proud to say that we still have many satisfied and loyal customers who have been with us since the beginning.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you locate underground utilities?

We use ground penetrating radar (GPR) and pipe/cable locators to locate underground utilities.

Can you locate utilities below the concrete slab?

We locate utilities embedded in the concrete as well as those below the concrete slab.

How do you locate water leaks?

We use advanced leak noise correlation computer systems as well as conventional acoustic listening devices.

Can you find PVC and other non-metallic utilities?

While metallic utilities are easier to locate, we do locate PVC, fiber optic, concrete and other non-metallic pipes/cables as well.
