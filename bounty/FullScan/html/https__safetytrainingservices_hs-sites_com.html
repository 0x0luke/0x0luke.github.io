"Blended Safety Services = Total Customer Value"

Training Services 

Our instructors are a group of safety and hazardous materials specialists who offer "hands-on" hazardous waste, emergency response, confined space entry, technical rescue, and other required training under simulated conditions as a practical alternative to "traditional" seminars. We offer training programs meeting the requirements of OSHA and where applicable, EPA/RCRA standards at either our 18,000 square foot training facility or the site of your choosing.

Field Services 

Highly trained confined space/industrial rescue standby teams can be deployed with a crew and associated equipment to your site in order to provide on-site rescue and retrieval of downed entrants to satisfy 1910.146 (k) requirements.

Technical Services 

Certified technicians provide safety equipment sales, rentals, and repairs. In addition, we provide fire extinguisher maintenance and inspection services.

Consulting Services

Whether you have had trouble with recent safety audits or are just looking for some help getting started, we have the training and knowledge you need to get the job done right.

Safety Training Services, Inc. offers a 50% discount program for active service military personnel and veterans for open enrollment safety training courses in Highland, Indiana. Must present a valid military ID, Veterans ID card, or DD-214. Use coupon code VET50 when registering to get your discount on courses such as Forklift Operator Training, Hazardous Materials Training, Confined Space Entry & Rescue Training, OSHA 10 and 30 Hour Training and more.
