Why Choose Us

Our dedicated team of experts makes sure that a resolution is achieved smoothly and efficiently allowing the executors to distribute the estate to the beneficiaries as quickly as possible.

Our fixed fee services offer our clients total transparency from the start and ensure more money left in the estate when it matters.

We successfully locate over 4,000 missing beneficiaries annually, across the UK and overseas. For added peace of mind we can assist you with putting in place Missing Beneficiary Indemnity Insurance.

With trusted contacts and specialist partners around the world, we know just where to look to find the information and people you need.

We maintain the highest ethical standards and uphold your valued business relationships. Your clients are your clients – we’ll simply be there when you need our help.

We’re proud to have been successfully solving complex estate administration cases for over half a century. There’s no doubt that today’s clients benefit from that experience and expertise every day.
