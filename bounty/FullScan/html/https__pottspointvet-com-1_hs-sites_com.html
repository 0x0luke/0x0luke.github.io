Offering Exceptional Pet Services to the Sydney Area

 

P

otts Point Veterinary Hospital provides advanced pet services, including allergy testing, behavioural training, cat boarding, and exotic pet care in the Sydney, Australia area. With more than 40 years of combined veterinary experience, we also offer all aspects of sick pet care, including cardiology, pain management, veterinary surgery, dentistry, blood tests, and hospitalization, with the latest in laboratory, radiology, ultrasonography, and dental equipment.

The staff of Potts Point Veterinary Hospital all live in the area, and are committed to the health and wellbeing of the loved pets of our friends and neighbours.

The Potts Point Veterinary Hospital veterinarians and staff keep you well informed through every stage of your pet's life, to ensure that it's health and happiness. We customise your animal’s wellness care according to his or her individual needs.

Our brand new state-of-the art facilities include separate dog and cat consulting rooms and wards, so our feline friends are never disturbed by the smell or sound of dogs, and a cat boarding area with a secure cat playroom.

For continuity of care, you and your pet will see the same veterinarian and nurse at each visit to Potts Point Veterinary Hospital.

We're proud of our modern, clean and spacious veterinary hospital. You can have a look for yourself with the new Google Inside. 

We also pride ourselves on getting to know each of our patients and clients personally - hope to see you soon!

 

Leptospirosis Virus

We have received many calls from worried fur parents regarding the recent news of the Leptospirosis virus.

The newspapers and Facebook have got the numbers a little wrong. Potts Point Vet Hospital have not seen any cases and following discussions with specialists vets & surroundings vet clinics in Sydney only 2 cases of Leptospirosis have actually been confirmed in Surry Hills and 1 in Glebe previously.
However, we acknowledge it is concerning and are offering a vaccination to our clients (that we have seen in the past 12 months) for just the cost of the vaccination at $18.
Please call the hospital on 8097 0060 if you would like to schedule an appointment.
