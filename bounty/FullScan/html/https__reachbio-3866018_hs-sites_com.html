ReachBio Research Labs provides primary cell biology based contract research services and products to the drug development and life science research communities.
Services

Our use of primary cell systems allows us to provide our clients with more predictable and clinically-relevant assays.

Products

Our extensive knowledge, and use of primary cell products allows us to confidently provide high quality primary cells from bone marrow, peripheral blood and cord blood.

News

The latest news, updates, and information from our lab in Seattle, WA.

With expertise encompassing many aspects of primary cell biology and a special focus on hematopoietic (blood and bone marrow) primary cell systems, we work with customers and clients involved in basic research through multiple areas of pre-clinical and clinical drug development.

Our platform is extensive and includes predictive and investigative toxicology, immuno-oncology, cell bank development, antibody testing, flow cytometry/cell sorting, assay development and much more.
