                              Frequently Asked Questions

                              How can I pause my box if I don't want to get it this week?
                              Just head to the deliveries area in your account, select the week you'd like to skip from the calendar and click 'skip week'. The date will turn red once the change has been made. Easy!

                              More

                              Is HelloFresh a subscription service?
                              We operate as a subscription service to take out the hard bits. All you need to do is cook and enjoy the delicious meals! We are a flexible service so you have the control to manage your deliveries: you can pause or cancel at any time!

                              More

                              What's in the box?
                              You'll receive easy-to-follow recipe cards with step-by-step instructions, as well as all the pre-measured ingredients you need to make each meal. Everything from pasta, meat and yoghurt to a bunch of herbs and a teaspoon of spice mix is included! All you need are a few pantry items.

                              More

                              How do I use my voucher?
                              Once you've selected the box you'd like to purchase you can input the voucher code in the box marked 'coupon' at the checkout. The discount will be applied against the final cost.
