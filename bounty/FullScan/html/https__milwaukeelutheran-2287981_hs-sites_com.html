We Teach Truth

The goal of all schools is to academically prepare their students for the challenges they will face in their future. That is a major focus of what we do, but MLHS is committed to something greater: preparing students for a lifelong relationship with Jesus Christ. Students have the opportunity to learn the Truth in a diverse environment where Christ is at the center of all our service to others.

Experience the difference that Jesus makes at our school.
