Comprised of more than 1,400 highly-trained and skilled journeymen and apprentices, the Northwest Ohio Piping Industry provides plumbing, pipefitting, welding, and HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) service to meet the residential and commercial needs throughout the 12 counties of Northwest Ohio.

We welcome you to look around our site and learn more about the hard-working men and women that make up the Piping Industry and the work we do.

We live in the communities where we work, and we are proud to do our part to improve Northwest Ohio.
