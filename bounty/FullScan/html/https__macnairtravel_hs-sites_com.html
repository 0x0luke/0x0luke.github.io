Are Your Travel & Entertainment Expenses
an Expense or an Investment?

Research shows that travel and entertainment is the 2nd largest controllable expense in businesses where travel is prevalent.  Additionally the travel incurred by organizations typically represents core parts of running and managing the organization successfully.  Yet, most mid-market  organizations treat travel as merely a necessary expense and fail to control the process to ensure succesful outcomes.  

An effective travel management process balances the needs of the organization and the needs of the travellers, ensuring the costs are minimized while performance is optimized.  With more than 30 years dedicated to managing travel programs, the team at MacNair Travel blends the tools, technology and process to turn travel from merely an expense to a driver of organizational success.
