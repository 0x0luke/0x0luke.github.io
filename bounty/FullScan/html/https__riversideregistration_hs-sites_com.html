About Us

Mey’s Insurance Services started off as a travel agency 16 years ago in La Puente, CA by Mey Aguilar, the owner of the business. In its humble beginning Mey’s Travel & Insurance (at that time) primarily derived its earnings from travel sales, but as the internet has grown, the travel sales have died down. Now the office offers several services, including insurance of many types, vehicle registration & title transfer service, notary public, tax preparation & even a VIN Verification service.
