Join a Skype Meeting or conference call from your PC

  1. In the Outlook calendar, open the meeting you want to join.
  2. In the meeting request, click Join Skype Meeting.

    A dilalog box will prompt you to choose how (or if) you want to join the audio portion.
  3. To connect to audio from your PC, in the Join Meeting audio dialog box, select the Use Skype for Business (full audio and video experience).

    ◦ The Call me at option gives you the option to have Skype call you at the number you select, or enter. When you answer the call, you will be connected to the meeting audio.
    When using the Call me at feature, use your complete phone number, including country code.

    ◦ Note: you can also manually set your audio settings.
  4. Click OK to join the meeting.

Join from an internal legacy EMC Cisco Video Room

To summarize, you will connect the room's hardware to the Skype Meeting, essentially as any attendee, by dialing the 'Video ID' from the invite.

Other in-room laptops can join the Skype meeting normally. However, since both the audio and in-room video will be handled by the room system, other in-room laptops should choose the 'Don't join Audio' option when prompted by Skype for Business. This will prevent any feedback or echoes. Everyone will be able to share content normally, displaying on the main room screen.

For Best Results, start the Skype Meeting from a Skype for Business client before joining the room to the meeting.
  1. In the meeting request, look for the Video ID . You will enter this number to dial in.
  2. From the console Directory List select Skype Meetings


  3. From the console select the Skype Meeting (SIP). entry.


    ◦ Note: if you are external to the EMC network, please see External Video Connections
  4. Tap the green “Call” button to ready the system to place the Skype call. This dials the video connection bridge ("Connect@video.emc.com").


  5. In the display, you must 'tab' over to 'Touch Tone' to bring up the keypad to enter the Video ID call number.
  6. Enter (dial) the Video ID from the invite, followed by the "#" key, into keypad when prompted.

  7. You will see the Video splashscreen on the main room monitor screen.


  8. The room will then connect to the Skype Meeting.
Tip: Reduce Feedback When joining a Skype Meeting from a room system, the meeting audio will come over the system speakers, so remember to choose “Don’t join Audio” or mute your computer speakers and microphone to limit feedback when joining from your laptop in the room. See "Joining laptops in conference rooms"

Join from an internal legacy EMC Telepresence Room

  1. In the Outlook calendar, open the meeting you want to join.
  2. In the meeting request, look for the Video ID . You will enter this number to dial in.
  3. From the console push the Directory button


  4. From the console select ! Skype Meeting. entry.

    ◦ Note: if you are external to the EMC network, please see External Video Connections
  5. Tap the “Call” button to bring up the keypad and place the call.
  6. You will see the Video Splash screen.


  7. Enter (dial) the Video ID (from the invite), followed by the "#" key, into keypad when prompted.

Tip: Reduce Feedback When joining a Skype Meeting from a room system, the meeting audio will come over the system speakers, so remember to choose “Don’t join Audio” or mute your computer speakers and microphone to limit feedback when joining from your laptop in the room. See "Joining laptops in conference rooms"

Join from internal legacy EMC Polycom HDX Video Room

  1. In the meeting request, look for the Video ID . You will enter this number to dial in.
  2. From the Remote Control, push the Directory button.

  3. Choose Favorites or Contacts directory entry and select the "Skype Meeting" entry.
  4. Press the "Dial" button on the remote to place the call.
  5. Enter (dial) the Video ID from the invite, followed by the "#" key into the remote control keypad.
Tip: Reduce Feedback When joining a Skype Meeting from a room system, the meeting audio will come over the system speakers, so remember to choose “Don’t join Audio” or mute your computer speakers and microphone to limit feedback when joining from your laptop in the room.

Joining by Phone (Audio Only)

  1. In the meeting request, you will see a list of Join by Phone numbers.
  2. Select and dial your local toll free number (preferred). Use the "Find local number" link from the invite if not listed. EMC Global Access Phone Number List
  3. When prompted, enter the Conference ID from the meeting invite, followed by the "#" key. You will be placed directly into the call, or the Audio Lobby until the host joins the meeting.
  4. This is also useful for dialing from a conference room Polycom phone.
Tip: Reduce Feedback When joining a Skype Meeting from a speaker phone, the meeting audio will come over the speakers, so remember to choose “Don’t join Audio” or mute the speakers and microphone on every computer in the room to limit feedback. See "Joining laptops in conference rooms"


Using the Skype Call-back Feature

  1. To have Skype call you back, in the Join Meeting audio dialog box, select the Call Me at option.

    ◦ The Call me at option gives you the option to have Skype call you at the number you select, or enter. When you answer the call, you will be connected to the meeting audio.

    ◦ Note: you can also manually set your audio settings.
  2. In the dropdown menu, select New Number to enter the phone number you want Skype to call.
    Be sure to use the complete phone number, including country code.
  3. This is also useful for dialing from a conference room Polycom phone.
Tip: Reduce Feedback When joining a Skype Meeting from a speaker phone, the meeting audio will come over the speakers, so remember to choose “Don’t join Audio” or mute the speakers and microphone on every computer in the room to limit feedback. See "Joining laptops in conference rooms"

Joining laptops in Conference Rooms

Join the laptop as you would any other Skype meeting (by clicking the link in the meeting invite, See Topic 1). However, if you are using the room's video (and therefore audio) equipment, it is important that all in-room laptops choose the "Don't join Audio" option when Skype prompts the audio choice. This will prevent feedback and echoes.


All in-room laptops will be able to share content normally through Skype. The content will be displayed on the main room screen.

  • No hard wired connections needed - Wireless enabled.
