Reflexion Email Security

Keep your users and your business safe with instant protection from email threats and data theft. The Reflexion range of easy-to-use cloud email security services ensure you meet regulatory compliance with services covering anti-spam and virus protection, encryption, archiving, and business continuity.

Become a Partner

Total Control Email Security

Reflexion’s anti-spam and email security service provides the configurability that IT solution providers need to address a wide range of customer requirements, with the automation and simplicity that end users love.

  • Blocks spam, viruses, and volume-based attacks before they reach the corporate network
  • Automatic inbound email queuing assures email continuity in the event of a local server outage
  • Outbound email filtering protects brand reputation and helps avoid IP address blacklisting

Email Archiving, Discovery and Recovery

Secure business data with an unlimited, searchable, securely stored cloud email archive that can be accessed at any time.

  • Rapid retrieval of emails that have been misplaced or deleted locally
  • An unlimited, searchable knowledge base of a company’s intellectual property stored in email
  • Robust eDiscovery features provide rapid means of responding to discovery requests for legal cases

Email Encryption

Protect your customers and yourself by encrypting sensitive email. Now you can easily ensure privacy and meet regulatory compliance with cost-effective email encryption.

  • Supports HIPAA email compliance, plus FINRA, GLBA, PHI, PII, and more
  • Seamless, secure, inbox-to-inbox communication with no change in end-user behavior
  • Force phrase keywords, encryption policy tripping, mark as confidential in Outlook

Email Continuity

A disruption to cloud email service providers can have a major impact on your business. Reflexion email continuity services allow end users to access their email and continue with business operations during an outage.

  • Allow users to send and receive email during an outage
  • 60-day rolling archive for email continuity during local server outages
  • Works with our filtering service for a complete business email solution
