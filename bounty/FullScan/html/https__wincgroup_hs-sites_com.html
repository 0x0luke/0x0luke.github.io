Strength through passion Stability through experience

We are a dedicated insurance agency committed to providing the proper level of protection and high level of personal service and attention.

 

The Winchester Group, founded by Gary Nichols in April 2002, is a full service independent insurance agency.

We are extremely proud of the experienced and highly qualified insurance professionals who have joined our agency since its inception. In addition, we have been able to secure contracts with some of the top insurance companies in our industry to include all of our product lines. Our services include consultation and marketing of business insurance, bonding, employee benefits, personal home and auto, business and individual life, individual health, long term disability and long term care.
