Welcome to Montrose Place Townhomes Member Services

Our Member Services portal represents single-point-of-contact for all residents living at “606”. This system offers each resident the ability to create a personalized account to submit and track any request made to the Association. This incident management system is monitored by the entire Association Management Team to facilitate transparent and effective communication between the Members, the Board of Directors, and our Community Manager.

If you are not ready to establish a personalized account today, you may contact us by phone at (888) 997-3955, or by email to Support@MontrosePlaceTownhomes.com

The following links will guide you to other on-line resource available to you as a resident.

About Us

Access information about the neighborhood, community, Association's Board of Directors, our mission, and information about meetings of the Membership.

Contact Us

Access information on how to contact the Association, the Management Company, or in the event of an emergency. If you have a personal emergency, please call 9-1-1.

Request Assistance

Create a request for support, ask a questions, voice a concerns, submit an Architectural Modification application, or report a violation. You can also make a request via our answering service by leaving a voice-message by calling Member Services at (281) 377-4443.

Community News

Access the Association community news & announcement blog. You can subscribe or bookmark the blog so you will be notified of future updates.

Governing Documents

Access the Associations Governing Documents, which includes the Articles of Incorporation, Declaration and Master Deed, ByLaws, and our Rules & Regulation and Fining policy.

Knowledge Base

Access a repository of information related to the community and this Membership. You will have to log-in or create a new account to access this information.

AssociaAccess® OnLine

Access your financial account online in real-time; see when your assessment was posted; review if charges have been assessed or reversed.

Leasing Resources

Access all resources provided by the Association related to Members leasing their units. This includes policy, procedures, and required documents.

MontrosePlaceTownhomes.com

Access the Associations main website where you can find a variety of community related information.
