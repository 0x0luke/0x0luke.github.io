INTELLIGENT POWER MODULES

You’ve used intelligent power modules before, but not like this. The OZipTM Intelligent Power Module offers the next level of versatility for whatever application you may build. An embedded Digital Signal Processor and a variety of application software, eliminate the need for external control hardware. That’s what makes the OZip a really intelligent power module.

MODULAR POWER CONVERSION FOR ENERGY STORAGE SYSTEMS

Distributed energy storage system design just got easier. With a compact, 2.5U form factor, the transformerless OZpcs-RS40 installs directly into Li-Ion battery racks. Front-side terminations simplify interconnect, and the fully parallelable, 40kW architecture permits easy system expansion. Offering wide 208-480VAC and 330-820VDC operating ranges, and design and certification for energy storage applications, system design doesn’t get any easier than with OZpcs.
