Increased phishing activity via email Subscribe
Update - We are continuing to work on a fix for this issue.
Aug 5, 10:31 BST
Identified - We have been seen an increased amount of compromised accounts and phishing emails circulating the UCA email system. Generally, the format of these emails appears to be that there is nothing in the body of the message except a line stating that the message has been 'shortened' or 'clipped'.

The confusing part about these emails which is the bit which could catch people out is that they appear to be from legitimate accounts, usually student ones. These accounts have likely been compromised and used to send out a mass email in the hope of catching people off guard due to the legitimate email address, but please do not be fooled, this is still a scam. There is a link in the email to 'Open full message', DO NOT CLICK THIS LINK.

If you receive an email such as this, please report it to the service desk team on extension 8799, or externally on 0330 102 7595, so that we can take steps to process the affected accounts.
Aug 5, 10:31 BST
