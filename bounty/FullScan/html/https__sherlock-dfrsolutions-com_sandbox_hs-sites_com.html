What Is Sherlock?


Sherlock cuts time and cost in making better, safer and more reliable products

Sherlock Automated Design Analysis™ software is a Physics of Failure (PoF) based electronics design reliability analysis tool that streamlines the new product development process by providing greater insights earlier, eliminating test failures due to design flaws and accelerating product qualification. Sherlock provides reliability predictions not otherwise possible and allows design iterations to be completed in hours – not weeks – resulting in reduced cost, risk and time to market.
