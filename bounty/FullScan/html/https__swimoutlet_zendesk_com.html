Why Shop at

Price Match Guarantee

We believe that our customers should always feel certain that they are receiving the best value from us. If within 24 hours of your purchase, you notice a lower list price for the same brand, item, size and color at another U.S. retail store online(see restrictions), just contact us and we will refund you the difference.

Low & Free Shipping

Unlike many other web sites that charge you huge shipping fees and increase them even more as your order goes up, we offer a flat $4.99 shipping fee. If you spend over $49, your order ships for FREE. If you spend over $99, your order ships FREE with 1-2 Day Delivery. (Some restrictions apply. Not all products are eligible for free shipping or instant credit. Not available for Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. Territories orders or select items.) View Shipping Options

Fast Shipping

We understand that getting your swim gear quickly is important to you, so we make every effort to process your order quickly. With the exclusion of certain specialty items shipped directly from the manufacturer, we typically ship all orders received by 6:00 PM EST the same business day.

Easy Returns

We want you to be 100% satisfied with every purchase you make at SwimOutlet.com. We will gladly accept eligible returns (see restrictions) in new and unused condition, with tags intact and packaging included within 30 days of the order shipment date for a full refund or store credit at your discretion. For full details, please see our Return Policy. Start a Return >

Largest Selection & Everything In-Stock

With over 100,000 different items in stock, we offer the best selection anywhere online. We add new styles to our site on a daily basis so that our selection is always fresh and up to date. If you are able to place an item into your shopping cart, that means that the item is available for immediate shipment, with the exclusion of some specialty items.
