RF Code for Core Data Centers

Improve Asset Utilization, Ensure Operational Efficiency, and Reduce Risk in Your Data Centers

Your data center is dynamic. Equipment is relocated, taken offline for maintenance, deployed or de-provisioned and can easily be misplaced. Environmental conditions can vary from rack to rack. Loss of equipment due to overheating turns into downtime and SLA violations you can’t afford. RF Code solutions provide real-time monitoring to lower costs and improve availability. 

  • Automated physical asset tracking
  • Real-time environmental and power monitoring
  • Protection against theft and SLA violations

RF Code for Edge

Real-Time Visibility and Real-Time Protection for Edge Computing

Edge Manager from RF Code gives you the continuous monitoring and the instant alerting necessary to safely and securely deploy edge locations, anywhere, worldwide. Manage your most remote and demanding edge computing environments with RF Code.

  • Real-time monitoring of environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, leak detection, and power usage
  • Continuous location tracking of all assets down to the rack
  • Asset protection through access monitoring
  • Location visibility via network camera edge-view system

RF Code for Internet of Things

Asset Location and Granular Environmental Monitoring Data for IoT Applications
