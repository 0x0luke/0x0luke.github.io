    About MODUL University Dubai

    The MODUL brand stands for more than 100 years of excellence in education. Founded in 1908, MODUL College is the vocational training school with the longest tradition in tourism & hospitality education worldwide.

    MODUL University is widely recognized as Austria's leading private international university and is owned by the Vienna Chamber of Commerce & Industry, the largest provider of private education in Austria.

    In September 2016, and after almost a century long legacy in Austrian education, MODUL’s education found a new home-outside-home in Dubai, UAE - with the inauguration of MODUL University Dubai on 10 Oct. 2016.

    Founded by Dubai Investments PJSC and DACH Advisory, MODUL University Dubai offers 360-degree higher education degrees in business, tourism, hospitality, sustainability, public governance and new media technologies, entrepreneurship and leadership, as well as a foundation programme and professional education short-courses.

    MODUL University Dubai, pays special attention to ensure innovative education, profound research, rich student experience, international partnerships and internship experience leading towards a successful job placement upon graduation.

    MODUL University Dubai is licensed by the Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA) of the Emirate of Dubai and all programmes are accredited by the University Quality Assurance International Board (UQAIB) and accredited by the Agency for Quality Assurance and Accreditation Austria (AQ Austria).
