About Kiwi Wealth

We are a 100% New Zealand owned and operated investment management business, helping Kiwis plan and invest for their futures through our KiwiSaver scheme, Superannuation scheme, Managed Funds and our Private Portfolio Service.

What we offer

If you’re looking to protect and grow your wealth, we have a range of investment solutions to suit different needs.

We manage the Kiwi Wealth KiwiSaver Scheme, Private Portfolio Service, Kiwi Wealth Super Scheme and the Kiwi Wealth Managed Funds.
