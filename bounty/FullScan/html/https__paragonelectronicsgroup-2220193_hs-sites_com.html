Is your manufacturing operation undermining business growth?

Outsourcing decisions can be as a result of a specific, short-term, operational need or as part of a forward-looking strategy. Perhaps you have outgrown your existing premises or assembly partner. Maybe you are struggling to recruit enough staff with the correct skills in order to keep ahead of the competition. Is further investment in plant and equipment really the right decision for your business? Consider for a moment if:
  • Your design and engineering team are spending too much time in the factory
  • You are unable to react to significant changes in customer demand
  • Quiet periods result in inefficient "down time"
  • Your primary skill sets are not in material procurement, assembly or test
  • You are more about design, innovation and customer service
  • You've struggled to deliver customer orders due to problems in the supply chain
