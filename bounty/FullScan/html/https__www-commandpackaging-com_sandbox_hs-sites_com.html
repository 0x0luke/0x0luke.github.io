Committed to Reducing Plastic Waste

Encore Recycling® partners with local growers to collect, wash, and recycle up to 100 million pounds of agricultural plastic annually, thus making it the industry leader in diverting this waste. Instead of going to landfills, this recycled material is used to create a continuous loop of sustainable plastic for our products. You reap the savings.
