Details

In the ever changing technological landscape there are many new born terms such as big data, smart mobility, anticipatory sensing, predictive analytics and many others. Each of those terms is due to the many applications that are enabledby the huge quantity of data generated everyday by people and electronic devices.

It is important to notice, that each dataset translates into data a certain action or more in general a certain activity that is monitored and registered. Thus each dataset in its diversity can tell us a small story related to the single aspect of the activity or phenomena the dataset itself represents. Those small stories are just like puzzle pieces. They can be combined by fusing the knowledge in each dataset to see the whole picture behind the puzzle and understand better why and how the single datasets relate or correlate to each other.
