Hello all,

My name is Ajinkya Domale. I completed my B.Tech in Electronics & Telecommunication from College of Engineering, Pune (CoEP). Currently I am working in Harman International as Associate Engineer, SW in Pune.

I was fond of reading since my childhood and I started writing in my 2nd year of engineering. At that time I used to write only poems and sometimes articles. But, my involvement in Spandan which is a social group from CoEP gave me a lot of lifetime experiences :) So, I started sharing those experiences on Facebook. Slowly & steadily people started liking my writings and it gave me confidence to write more. I tried and people appreciated :D

Some of the people always used to comment on my Facebook posts that I write too much :P :D Also, my posts used to get lost in the news feed stuff of Facebook and many people didn't even come to know that I wrote something!

So, here am I :) Blogging for the first time. Right now I have archived some of my popular Facebook posts but soon I will be updating my blog :)  Stay connected, have a happy reading :)

For any feedback & suggestions, feel free to contact me at domaleajinkya@gmail.com
