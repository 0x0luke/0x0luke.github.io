What's The Point?

The Point is a café at 505 Brannan Street, at Pinterest HQ in San Francisco, featuring Pin-worthy eats and drinks, prepared by a talented team of coffee artisans.

Limited-edition products such as enamel pins, stickers, diner mugs and beanies are also available.

Hours

 

Open Monday - Friday from 8:00 a.m. until 3:30 p.m.
