Airlift (USA), Inc. is a global logistics Company offering transportation and supply chain management solutions. We offer freight forwarding services to customers worldwide.

The Company is headquartered in the USA and has 500 employees worldwide". At Airlift we measure our success by yours. Everyday our people go to work to help our customers succeed. From Delhi to Detroit, from Madras to Minneapolis, our job is channeling knowledge and value through lines of service and industry-specialized practices.

We employ trained professionals in service centers worldwide; these offices are linked into a seamless network through an integrated information management system. Services include air and ocean freight forwarding, vendor consolidation, marine insurance, distribution and other value added global logistics services.

Airlift is a licensed Ocean Transportation Intermediary (OTI) and is uniquely positioned to handle all aspects of your shipment from start to finish. We offer a full range of ocean related services including Ocean Forwarding, NVOCC Consolidation or FCL container management as well as other services. We specialize in ocean consolidation from the Far East with purchase order visibility and monitoring, vendor compliance, timeliness, consolidation and customs loading.
