Support Hours

Submit a request

SF SkiFest customer support is available M-F 9am-5pm Pacific. We try to answer all requests within one business day.

If you need assistance via a phone call please leave your number and a good time to reach you during business hours on the contact form and we will be happy to give you a call back.

If you can't find what you need, please feel free to submit a request using the link above and we'll reply within 24 hrs, Monday - Friday. If you email us Saturday - Sunday, a representative will get back to you Monday.
